Cladoselache & Doliodus

  • Protolithic Puncture: Tankbuster.
  • Tidal Guillotine: AoE attack centered around Cladoselache.

Trash B

  • Mixed results on pulling everything.

Marquis Morbol

  • Arbor Storm: Party-wide damage.
  • Extensible Tendrils: Four column attack that slowly rotates.
  • Blossom: Persistent AoE that debuffs players.
  • Sap Shower: Circular attack targeting all non-Tank players.


Trash C

  • Can interrupt some of the abilities.

Guardian Force: Quetzalcoatl

  • Shockbolt: Tankbuster.
  • Thunderstorm: Dodge the AoEs, then pick up the leftover electrical orbs for a damage boost. Otherwise they buff the boss.
  • Winding Current: Knocks you to opposite side of arena. Save mobilities for this to dodge AoE.
