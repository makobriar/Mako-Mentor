Trash A

  • Voidcall summons new mobs. Kill that caster first.

Psycheflayer

  • Kill Lunatic Priest first.
  • Interrupt Void Thunder III as much as possible.
  • Burn down adds ASAP.
  • You can pull boss to front or back of room for easy AoE avoidance.


Trash B

  • Statues spawn adds.
  • Get close enough to statues to aggro adds.

Demon Wall

  • Don't fall off!
  • Liquefy: Columns on middle, then sides.
  • Repel: Stack in middle


Trash C

  • Succubi have AoE fireballs.

Anantaboga

  • Tank boss next to northeast statue with his butt against wall.
  • Watch for Disease and cleanse it.
  • Imminent Catastrophe: Hide behind statue. Room-wide AoE.
  • Adds spawn after Imminent Catastrophe. Burn 'em down.
  • Tethers: Run away from party. Drops lingering AoE.
