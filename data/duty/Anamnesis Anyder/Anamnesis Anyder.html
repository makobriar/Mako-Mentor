Trash A


Unknown

  • Fetid Fang: Tankbuster.
  • Try to angle the boss sideways. He does a frontal and rear cone.
  • Scrutiny: Rotating cone AoEs with minimal safe spots.
  • Luminous Ray: Cone on targeted player. Look for fiery orb in its mouth.


Trash B


Kyklops

  • Terrible Hammer/Blade: Grids marked with weapons. Stand on the last weapon that appears, they blow up in order.
  • Raging Glower: Column on random player. Telegraphs by hands on his face.
  • Open Hearth: Stack marker on a random player.


Trash C


Rukshs Dheem

  • Bonebreaker: Tankbuster.
  • Depth Grip: Hands slam where they're facing in order. Get away from them.
  • Unnatural Ripples: Stand on the vents with spraying water. Otherwise an add will spawn.
  • Kill adds that tether to her.
  • Flying Fount: Stack on a random player
